Living Memories

To record, recollect, represent, and reconstruct our stories.

Keyword

  • Nostalgia
  • Interactive storytelling
  • GIS database

Background

With decades of urbanization, old buildings were torn down while new streets, communities, or even towns come into being. Civil construction and population movement have forever reshaped the living environment in only half-centuries. The cover image is just solid proof that dramatic changes took place in my hometown. [Learn More] However, The rapid development seems doesn't leave a place for memory and nostalgia. I feel so deeply when the old generation trying to tell their stories with lacking the source. The plain description conveys limited information to the young generation as they never experienced and cannot understand the significance.

Hongzhen Old Town - A vividly fading case in Shanghai.


Approach

Living memories is an open-source platform dedicated to preserving to memorize daily life for everyone. It is as a combination of Wikipedia, google Map, and Instagram -- A database integrated with social media and interested-location centered. The user could upload personal memories as stories or photography and link to location or time. By plotting in a special view order or event/location cluttering, the potential connection might be revealed while the pieces of stories would figure a holistic reconstruction as a whole.

The famous locations like the city center, historical heritage sites, remarkable sites, are most attractive and with data enrichment in data could be designed as a crucial sign for evoking. Besides, public storytelling with its own memories would also magnify the enjoyment of nostalgia and recognize the past time.


User Scenario

Ordinary people. They could be our friend, parents, who want to narrate life stories and cherish old-good moments.

Historical researcher or experts, those often challenged by labeling geography Infos, the changing of administrative regions, they would have a powerful tool. [Example:探索舟山地名背后的故事很精彩]

Professional curators, planning for museums or exhibitions to demonstrate the changes and developments, could create a digital copy of artifacts.[Example:在历史跨越的现场——纪念改革开放40周年中国民族影像志摄影展]

Sharing contents to rural area with digital format.


Role of Narrative

Another interesting point is the view of the narrative. Occasionally, it is an official institution that dominated the historical narrative. They gathering sources and show in a grand mainstream theme, like revolution history, massive urban planning projects, and so on. Sometimes, it is far away from folklife and becoming unattractive. Perhaps they are more personal and down to earth sight. This project might provide a time machine, for them to create their history.
