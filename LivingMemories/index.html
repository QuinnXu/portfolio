Living Memories

To record, recollect, represent and reconstruct our stories.

Keyword

  • Nostalgia
  • Interactive storytelling
  • GIS database

Background

With decades of urbanization, old buildings were teared down while new street, commuties or even towns come into being. Civil construction and populuation movement have forever reshaped the living environment in only half centeries. The cover image is just a solid proof that dramatic changes took place on my hometown. [Learn More] However, The rapid development seems doesn't leave a place for memory and nostalgia. I feel so deeply when old generation trying to tell there stories with lacking of source. The plain description conveys limited information to young generation as they never experienced and cannot understand the significance.

Hongzhen Old Town - A vividly fading case in Shanghai.


Approach

Living memories is open source platform dedicated to preserve to memorize dailylifes for everyone.It could be regarded as a combination of Wikipedia, google Map and Instalgram -- A datebase integrated with social media and interested-location centered. User could upload personal memories as stoies or photography and link to location or time. By plotting in a certain view order or event/location cluttering, the potential connection might be revealed while the pieces of storie would figure a holistic reconsstruction as a whole.

The famouse locaitons,e.g. city center,historical hertage sites, remarkable buildings, which is most attractive and with data richment in data could be designed as key milestone for recalling.Besides, public storytelling with personally memories would also enhance the feelling of nosgalia and identify the past time.


User Scenario

Ordinary people. They could be our friend, parents, who want to tell life stories and chrish old good times.

Historical reseacher or experts, those often challenged by labelling geography infos, the changing of administration regions. [Example:探索舟山地名背后的故事很精彩]

Profesionnaly curators, planning for Museum or exhibitions to demontrate the changes and developments. [Example:在历史跨越的现场——纪念改革开放40周年中国民族影像志摄影展]

Sharing contents to rural area with digital format.


Role of Narrative

Another interesting point is the view of narrative. Occationally, it is offical institution that dominant the historical narrative. They gathering source and show in a grand mainstream theme, like revolution history, massive urban planning projects and so on. Sometime, it is far away from folk life and becoming unattractive. Perhaps they are more personal and down to earth sight. This project might provide a time machine like tool, for them to create own history.
